AuraAttend User Guide

A complete walkthrough of features, AI timetable scanning, skip-day prediction algorithms, and teacher verification systems.

🚀 Launch Web Application 📖 Jump to Quick Start

📊 1. Student Dashboard & Live Statistics

Track your overall percentage, manage subject cards, and instantly log daily attendance.

📍 Realtime Interface: Student main dashboard showing overall 88% attendance, color-coded status badges, and direct Present/Absent controls.
📈

Overall Attendance Progress

Displays total present count vs total classes conducted across all subjects in your active semester.

  • Green Badge: Meeting or exceeding your target percentage (e.g. ≥ 75%).
  • Red Badge: Falling below your target threshold.
⚡

One-Tap Attendance Buttons

Mark attendance in a single tap directly from your subject list:

  • ✓ Present: Increments present and total class counts.
  • ✗ Absent: Increments absent count and total class counts.
  • 🌴 Holiday: Logs class cancellation without penalizing your percentage.

📸 2. AI Timetable & Routine Scanner

Upload a photo or screenshot of your college timetable to build your schedule automatically.

📍 Realtime Interface: AI Gemini OCR Review Wizard displaying extracted slots with custom time pickers and group filters.
🤖

Google Gemini AI OCR

Scans routine images (PNG, JPG, JPEG) and automatically identifies:

  • Subject Code & Full Name
  • Class Type (`LECTURE` vs `LAB`)
  • Day of the Week (`MONDAY` – `SUNDAY`)
  • Start & End Times in 24-hour format
🛠️

Interactive Slot Review Wizard

Review and edit extracted slots in a clean popup modal before saving:

  • Group Filtering: Filter out irrelevant lab groups (e.g. keep Group A1, exclude Group A2).
  • Time Pickers: Fine-tune class timings using native click selectors.
  • + Add Slot: Insert missed classes or custom extra lectures.

🔮 3. Skip-Day Advisor & Bunk Simulator

Know exact safe skip counts and simulate future class decisions in real time.

📍 Realtime Interface: Analytics Visualizer featuring the Skip-Day Advisor card, Bunk Predictor Slider, and Recharts performance graph.
💡

Smart Skip Advice Engine

Calculates exact attendance recommendations based on your target threshold (75% or 60%):

  • Safe to Skip: "You can safely skip the next 2 classes."
  • Must Attend: "You must attend the next 3 classes to reach 75%."
🎛️

Multi-Day Bunk Simulator Slider

Drag the interactive slider to simulate skipping upcoming classes. See live percentage projections updated instantly on screen.

📐 Mathematical Calculation Formulas

AuraAttend uses strict mathematical models to ensure accurate skip predictions:

Max Bunk Allowed = Floor[ ( Present - (Target * Total) ) / Target ]
Classes Needed to Recover = Ceiling[ ( (Target * Total) - Present ) / (1 - Target) ]

🍎 4. Teacher & Security Verification Portal

Allow professors to view and update student records using secure 4-digit PINs.

📍 Realtime Interface: Teacher portal showing Student Unique Code lookup, 4-digit Edit PIN security verification, and batch logging controls.
🔑

Rotable Security PIN

Students can generate a temporary 4-digit Teacher Edit PIN (e.g. `4921`). Teachers enter this PIN along with the student's unique code to log attendance safely.

👥

Batch Operations

Teachers can paste multiple comma-separated student codes (e.g. `STU-101, STU-102, STU-103`) to unlock and update attendance records simultaneously.

📖 5. Quick Start Guide for New Users

Follow these 4 easy steps to start using AuraAttend in under 2 minutes.

1

Create Your Account

Visit AuraAttend Web App, click Sign Up, enter your email and password, and type your initial semester name (e.g., "4th Semester").

2

Add Schedule or Upload Routine Photo

Go to the Timetable tab and click Upload Routine Image for automatic AI scanning, OR go to Subjects → click + Add Subject to enter subject names manually.

3

Log Daily Classes

After each lecture or lab, tap Present (✓) or Absent (✗) on the corresponding subject card.

4

Check Analytics & Skip Safety

Open the Analytics tab to review your performance charts, check your skip safety scores, and export CSV history logs.

❓ 6. Frequently Asked Questions

Does AuraAttend work offline?

Yes! AuraAttend is a Progressive Web App (PWA). If your device loses internet connection, your attendance logs are cached in your browser and automatically uploaded when connectivity returns.

How do holidays affect my score?

Marking a class as "Holiday" removes that class session from total class counts entirely, ensuring canceled classes never penalize your score.

Can I share my schedule with friends?

Yes! Click Share Routine under the Timetable tab to copy your unique schedule code. Your classmates can paste this code to import your timetable instantly.

How do I export my attendance history?

Open the History tab and click Export CSV to download a complete spreadsheet report of all your logged attendance sessions.
